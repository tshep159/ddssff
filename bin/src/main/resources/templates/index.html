    Spelling Kay Campaign

    The Spelling Kay is a campaign whereby learners above 15 years register on to participate in writting 10 given words and stand a chance of winning R200 daily for just R20 per entry, the students will be given the words via audio (and their meanings) to write. It consists of only One (1) stage. Kindly note that participants are not competing against each other whoever get all the words correct will receive their prices respectively and prices will only be issued out to students who got all their 10 words correct.

    Prices are allocated as follows

    Prices are allocated directly into your given account automatically after succesfuly completing your 10 words and you can use the funds to sponsor any of your educational, business or personal needs at home.
